OUR PROJECTS

Promotion of education among downtrodden class

We have picked up the education of girls because if we teach one man we teach one person but if we teach one woman we teach a family and an educated family turns into an educated nation ultimately. Then it will be easy for us to awake these educated children to make them ready and rise for the promotion of plantation. An educated person can easily understand that trees are our ultimate and selfless friend without trees our sustenance is totally impossible. Now we are picking areas one by one like Chitrakoot for plantation which will go large in a long run.

Tree Plantation

Family welfare foundation is currently working in the entire bundelkhand area towards various social causes. We are planting trees in the different districts and creating awareness to protect the mother earth by being environment friendly. We can never thank a tree enough for its contribution. Rather than thanking, we have started planting tree globally with help of our team. Donate for a sapling and be a hero of your environment.

Free Health check up camps.

A good health is the biggest wealth of a human being, to maintain this wealth we organize timely free health check up camps for the underprivileged. Support us to keep the momentum going. Free Medical Camps at FWF are organized every quarter to raise awareness about important healthcare issues, encourage residents to adopt health practices and to improve patient health. Expert doctors and practitioners provide free of cost consultations and conduct awareness sessions.
