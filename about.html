ABOUT US

Family Welfare Foundation (FWF) is a non-profit organization in Pakistan with Globally registered Chapters in USA, UK, Australia, & South Africa. Our aim is to improve the conditions of the underprivileged communities of our society in terms of Education, Healthcare Services, Access to Water, Sports Rehabilitation and Emergency Relief Response.

Our NGO is completely dedicated to the mankind irrespective of cast, creed and religion. Poverty is not only a problem but it's a deep rooted stigma in our society. Poor people not only struggle for their daily bread but they also suffer with neglect and marginalization from the main stream of the society. We help poor people in getting food, health and education.
